Vegetables are nutrient-rich edible parts of plants, including leaves, roots, stems, and flowers. They are packed with essential vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and fiber, supporting overall health and well-being. Regular consumption of vegetables helps boost immunity, improve digestion, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes. They come in a variety of colors, each offering unique health benefits—leafy greens for iron and calcium, orange vegetables for vitamin A, and cruciferous vegetables for cancer-fighting properties.
