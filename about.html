Questions and Answers about Vegetables

1. Why are vegetables important for health?

Vegetables are rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They help boost immunity, improve digestion, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and diabetes.

2. What is the most nutritious vegetable?

Spinach is often considered one of the most nutritious vegetables because it is packed with vitamins A, C, K, iron, and calcium.

3. Can vegetables help with weight loss?

Yes, vegetables are low in calories and high in fiber, making them an excellent choice for weight loss. They help you feel full without adding extra calories.

4. How many servings of vegetables should I eat daily?

It is recommended to eat at least 5 servings of vegetables per day as part of a balanced diet.
